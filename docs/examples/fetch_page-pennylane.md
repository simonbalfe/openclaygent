Their biggest event of the year: the target was between 6 and 9 meetings per AE. They hit 15.

The National Congress of Chartered Accountants is Pennylane’s single biggest lead moment of the year. It’s where nearly half of the team’s leads in this segment originate, making it a critical moment for pipeline generation.Each Account Executive needed to book between 6 and 9 meetings ahead of the conference.But there was a problem.

Each AE manages portfolios of 200–300 accounts, and reaching out manually to that many contacts before a major event simply isn’t realistic.

That’s where the team rethought their entire preparation process.

Instead of relying on manual outreach, the Growth Ops team prepared everything in advance:

-   pre-built outreach sequences,
    
-   segmented prospect lists inside Salesforce,
    
-   and messaging tailored specifically for the event.
    

Once everything was ready, AEs didn’t need to build campaigns themselves.

They simply selected the right accounts from Salesforce, slightly customized the messaging when needed, and launched the sequences.

The key enabler was the lemlist Chrome extension.

From Salesforce, AEs could enroll hundreds of contacts into sequences in just a few clicks, without leaving their workflow or juggling multiple tools.

In practice, that meant:

→ Ops handled the structure,→ AEs stayed focused on selling,→ and outreach could scale instantly.

Instead of hitting the initial target of 6 to 9 meetings, some AEs ended up with 15 meetings booked before the event even started.
